A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Providence, Rhode Island, with my small growing family. My journey into tech started with a degree in journalism. As I started sharing my writing online, I was fascinated with how easily I could get my voice out there. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to tailor my specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned to how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versaility I was looking for. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML
